📖 Unit 1：生活中的圆与扇 (Circles & Sectors in Life) ▼

Unit 1 Exercise 单元一练习 自测智能体 - 圆与扇形

Part 1 – Understanding Circles ▼

  • u1-w1-l1: 我能识别圆心、半径、直径，并测量圆形物体，正确使用数学术语。 (I can identify the center, radius, and diameter of a circle and measure circular objects to correctly use mathematical terminology.)
    ★★★未评分
    Practical Measurement: Quickly measure and tell me the approximate diameter of a round object. (请快速测量一个圆形物体并告诉我这个物体的大致直径。)
    True or False: 'The diameter is the longest chord in a circle. (True/False)' (直径是圆中最长的弦。 对/错).
    How to find the center of a circle? 如何找到一个圆的圆心？
  • u1-w1-l2: 我能用不同的方式测量圆的面积与周长，推导周长与面积公式。 (I can measure the area and circumference of a circle using different methods and derive the formulas for circumference and area.)
    ★★★未评分
    What is the relationship between the circumference formula (C = πd) and the area formula (A = πr²)? Can you explain how they are connected conceptually? (周长公式 C = πd 和面积公式 A = πr² 之间有什么联系？你解释它们的关联吗？)
    If you double the radius of a circle, what happens to its circumference? What happens to its area? (Double, triple, quadruple?) (如果一个圆的半径变为原来的两倍，它的周长会如何变化？面积呢？ 变为两倍、三倍还是四倍？)
    A circle has an area of 25π cm². What is its radius? What is its circumference? (一个圆的面积是25π 平方厘米。它的半径是多少？周长是多少？)
  • u1-w1-l3: 我能将圆的公式应用到车轮、跑道等真实情境。 (I can apply formulas of cicle/sector to real-world situations like wheels and running tracks.)
    ★★★未评分
    A bicycle wheel has a diameter of 70 cm. How far will the bicycle travel if the wheel makes 100 complete revolutions? (Show your reasoning) (一个自行车轮的直径是70厘米。如果车轮转了100整圈，自行车前进了多少距离？)
    A standard running track has two straight sections and two semi-circular ends. If the total length of the straight sections is 80m and the diameter of each semicircle is 40m, what is the total distance around one lap of the track? (一个标准跑道有两条直道和两个半圆弯道。如果直道总长80米，每个半圆的直径是40米，跑一圈的总长度是多少？)
    A small pizza has a diameter of 20 cm and costs ¥40. A large pizza has a diameter of 30 cm and costs ¥60. Which pizza gives you more area for your money? Justify your answer. (一个小披萨直径20厘米，卖40元。一个大披萨直径30厘米，卖60元。买哪个披萨更划算？请证明你的选择。)
    The minute hand on a clock is 10 cm long. What is the area of the sector that the minute hand sweeps through in 15 minutes? (一个钟表的分针长10厘米。分针在15分钟内扫过的扇形面积是多少？)
评分已保存！

Part 2 – Circle Area ▼

  • u1-w2-l1: 我能将圆形/扇形面积公式应用到盘子、杯盖等实际对象，解决面积问题。 (I can apply the area formula of circle or sector to real objects like plates and cup lids.)
    ★★★未评分
    一个餐盘直径24厘米。盘子上有一个距离外缘2厘米的装饰环。这个装饰环的面积是多少？
  • u1-w2-l2: 我能建立弧长、圆心角与半径的联系，并在题目中运用。 (I can connect arc length, central angle, and radius and use them in problems.)
    ★★★未评分
    写出弧长公式。如果圆心角扩大两倍而半径不变，弧长会如何变化？一段弧长为10π厘米，其所对的圆心角为72°。求这个圆的半径。
评分已保存！

Part 3 – Sectors & Data ▼

  • u1-w3-l1: 我能用百分比计算扇形面积，联系分数、小数、百分数与几何。 (I can calculate sector area using percentages and connect fractions/decimals/percentages with geometry.)
    ★★★未评分
    一个圆形花园被分区。如果25%的面积种了玫瑰，且花园总半径是6米，求玫瑰种植区的面积。
  • u1-w3-l2: 我能收集数据，转换为百分比与圆心角，并绘制饼图。 (I can collect data, convert to percentages and angles, and create pie charts.)
    ★★★未评分
    一项调查中，30个学生里有12人最喜欢足球。在饼图中，“足球”部分的圆心角应该是多少度？提供一个简单数据集：最喜欢的颜色：红=10, 蓝=15, 绿=5。请计算每种颜色对应的圆心角。
  • u1-w3-l3: 我能解读真实图表并分析其反映的信息与故事。 (I can read real data charts and analyze the stories they tell.)
    ★★★未评分
评分已保存！

Unit 1 Assessment Rubrics - Pie Chart of My Life 评分标准

Criteria 标准 Excellent 优秀 (4) Good 良好 (3) Satisfactory 合格 (2) Needs Improvement 需改进 (1)
Ratios & Accuracy
比例与准确性		 Ratios/percentages are calculated correctly and add up to 100%. Segments are drawn precisely to match the data.

比例或百分比计算正确，总和为100%，图表与数据完全对应。		 Ratios/percentages are mostly correct; chart is mostly accurate (小错误)。 Some mistakes in calculations or chart does not match the ratios clearly.

计算有错误，或图表与比例不清楚对应。		 Ratios missing or incorrect; chart does not reflect data.

比例缺失或错误，图表与数据不符。
Use of Math Concepts
数学概念运用		 Clear use of fractions, ratios, or percentages. Shows understanding of how data translates into the chart.

能清楚使用分数、比例或百分比，理解数据如何转化为图表。		 Mostly shows fractions/ratios/percentages with some small gaps in clarity.

大部分能体现分数、比例或百分比，但有些不够清晰。		 Limited use of math concepts; unclear how numbers were used.

数学概念运用有限，不清楚数字是如何应用的。		 Little or no evidence of ratios or percentages.

几乎没有比例或百分比的体现。
Aesthetics
美观		 Very neat, colorful, and well-organized. Clear labels and easy to read.

非常整洁，色彩丰富，布局清晰，标签明确，容易阅读。		 Neat and organized with some effort in design. Labels are mostly clear.

整洁有序，设计有一定用心，标签基本清楚。		 Somewhat neat but messy in parts; labels are incomplete or unclear.

有一定整洁度，但部分凌乱，标签不完整或不清楚。		 Minimal effort; messy, hard to read, or incomplete.

几乎没有用心，凌乱难读，或不完整。
Creativity
创意		 Unique, creative design; adds personal touches (符号、插图或主题)。 Some creative elements included.

有一些创意元素。		 Limited creativity; chart looks plain.

创意有限，图表较普通。		 No creativity; chart is incomplete or very basic.

没有创意，图表不完整或非常简单。
Meaning & Reflection
意义与反思		 Categories are thoughtful and meaningful. Reflects personal life and priorities clearly.

分类有深思熟虑且有意义，能清楚反映个人生活与重点。		 Categories make sense and show some thought.

分类合理，体现一定思考。		 Categories are basic or generic; limited personal connection.

分类较为简单或普通，个人联系有限。		 Categories unclear or random; does not show personal meaning.

分类模糊或随意，缺乏个人意义。

📖 Unit 2: 比与比例 (Ratio & Proportion) ▼

Unit 2 Exercise 单元二练习 AI自测助手-比与比例

Part 1 – Ratios and Proportions ▼

  • u2-w1-l1: 我能理解比的意义，并能正确读写比、求比值。 (I can understand the meaning of ratios, and can correctly read, write, and calculate the value of ratios.)
    ★★★未评分
    一个班有15个男生和20个女生。写出男生与女生的比，并求出比值。(A class has 15 boys and 20 girls. Write the ratio of boys to girls and find its value.)
  • u2-w1-l2: 我能理解并应用比的基本性质，化简比。 (I can understand and apply the basic properties of ratios to simplify them.)
    ★★★未评分
    化简比 2.5 : 1.5。(Simplify the ratio 2.5 : 1.5.)
评分已保存！

Part 2 – Proportional Reasoning ▼

  • u2-w2-l1: 我能理解比例的意义和基本性质，并能解比例。 (I can understand the meaning and basic properties of proportions and solve them.)
    ★★★未评分
    解比例：x/4 = 9/12。(Solve the proportion: x/4 = 9/12.)
  • u2-w2-l2: 我能判断两个相关联的量成什么比例关系，并能解决实际问题。 (I can determine the proportional relationship between two related quantities and solve practical problems.)
    ★★★未评分
    一辆汽车行驶的路程和时间成什么比例？如果2小时行驶120千米，5小时能行驶多少千米？(What is the proportional relationship between the distance a car travels and time? If it travels 120 km in 2 hours, how far can it travel in 5 hours?)
评分已保存！

Part 3 – Scale and Similarity ▼

  • u2-w3-l1: 我能理解图上距离与实际距离的比就是比例尺，并能解决相关问题。 (I can understand that the ratio of map distance to actual distance is the scale and solve related problems.)
    ★★★未评分
    在一幅比例尺是1:500000的地图上，两地相距4.5厘米，实际距离是多少千米？(On a map with a scale of 1:500000, the distance between two places is 4.5 cm. What is the actual distance in kilometers?)
  • u2-w3-l2: 我能应用比例知识解决图形的放大与缩小问题。 (I can apply knowledge of proportion to solve problems of enlarging and reducing figures.)
    ★★★未评分
    一个长方形长6厘米，宽4厘米，按2:1的比放大后，长和宽分别是多少？面积是原来的几倍？(A rectangle is 6 cm long and 4 cm wide. After being enlarged by a ratio of 2:1, what are its new length and width? How many times larger is the new area?)
评分已保存！
